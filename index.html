Experience & Credibility

Based in Silicon Valley, our team has lived and built through every wave of modern technology — from dot com internet, search, mobile, cloud, fintech, and now AI.

We know how to spot what lasts and what fades.

Our team consists of engineers and designers who became product managers and executives.

We have scaled consumer businesses reaching 500M+ monthly active users, directly managed 130+ reports and $1.6B in annual revenue.

We built and scaled core developer platforms — from pre-IPO to today’s global scale — including identity, APIs, and SaaS products used by millions. We also pioneered credit card tokenization and modern digital payments during the height of the “wallet wars.”

Then we moved from scale to entrepreneurship. We raised $31M+, created a new financing model, and put 500+ electric vehicles on the road, avoiding over 9,000 metric tons of CO₂.

Today, through the Bell AI Fellowship, we bring technologists and creators together to explore the future of human-AI interaction. In parallel, we are building Zarû, AI-native infrastructure designed for a new class of entrepreneurs. We are also exploring TaskCloud, a next-generation platform for learning and productivity

That mix is what makes us different:

  • Operators of billion-dollar businesses
  • Entrepreneurs building from zero
  • Advisors helping others shape what's next
  • Technologists who've lived through every era of modern computing

This is the foundation of Aught Hour: a platform for building new AI ideas and guiding others through the opportunities — and risks — of this era of change.
